Official Scramble Rules

  • Each team member tees off, and the best shot is selected.
  • All players then play their next shot from that spot.
  • This process continues until the ball is holed.
  • Teams must record one score per hole.
  • Handicaps must be submitted honestly when creating a profile.
  • Sportsmanship and fair play are expected at all times.
