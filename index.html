Our Mission

At Scramble Boys, our mission is to bring golfers together through fun, competitive, and fair scramble tournaments. We want to create a community where players of all skill levels can meet new people, enjoy the game, and keep the spirit of friendly competition alive.

Tournament Rules

  • Scramble format will be followed at all events.
  • Each player must create a profile including age and handicap.
  • After rounds, partners can rate each other for sportsmanship and play.
  • Teams decide on buy-in, course, and pot before each event.
  • All players are expected to respect the game, the course, and each other.
